I am absorbed on the net

Seeing the whole world

Yet chained I remain

And watch the time go

Ignoring family and friends

To live my life virtual

I truly remain unhappy

Trapped in this fake realm

At last my phone goes out

Freeing me of this burden

Bringing light to my soul

To show me the real world
