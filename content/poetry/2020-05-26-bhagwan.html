Hum naachate hain uske ishaaron par

Azad hain hum nahi

Issme hain humare khushi

Kyon ki woh ho sabase mahaan

Hum usse mahasoos nahi kar paate

Phir bhee woh hai yahin

Surarakshit hain hum tabhee

Jab tak hain uske saath
