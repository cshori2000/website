Dungeons of stress chain me

Eating my freedom away

At last I take time to relax

To ease my ghastly pain

And healing my lasting wounds

Now letting me be away of fray

The calm soothes my soul

Making me feel reborn

I feel ready to go out

And face challenges of the world
