2020

June 3, 2020   

The summer had begun

It is time to have fun

Yet the virus still roams

And peace has gone fray

Society is in chaos

Leaders deaf to their pleas

Change must soon come

Or face the angry kin

Action is now needed

To have justice served

It is time to wake up

To have our voiced heard
