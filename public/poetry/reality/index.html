Reality

May 30, 2020   

The peace of silence is all but forgotten

Giving time for me to ponder my thoughts

Allowing me to see what is not noticed

Bringing light to what I thought was gone

I now see the truth of my world’s reality

No longer do I remain so blind to atrocity

Now is my time to take a form action

And show the world the the power of unity
